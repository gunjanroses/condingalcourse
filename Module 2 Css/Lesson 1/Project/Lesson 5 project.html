My Fitness Routine Cards

This shows the different kinds of workout to do in daily life to stay healthy.

Morning Stretch

After u wake up to freshen up do light stretches for ur arms,body,neck,back and feel lighter.

Time: 15 minutes

Cardio Workout

Do heavy exercises for each part which makes u tired and increases ur stamina and energy everytime u do it.

Time: 45 minutes

Strength Training

Practise squats, push-ups, and planks to build ur body strength.

Time: 50 minutes

Yoga Time

Try simple yoga poses to improve focus,better breathing,feeling lighter and flexibility.

Time: 20 minutes

Evening Walk

Go for a small walk and enjoy the surroundings screen-free and enjoy the nature,breeze,etc.

Time: 10 minutes

Rest and Hydrate

Exercising atlast 5 days a week is more than necessary rest the rest 2 days and keep ur body rested drink enohg water since that also helps with weight-loss.

Time: Any 2 days in the week

Fitness Tip: Staying consistent,eating healthy and hydrating enough with good hygiene helps a ton.
